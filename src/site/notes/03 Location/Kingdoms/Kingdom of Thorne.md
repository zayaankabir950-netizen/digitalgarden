> [!abstract] Kingdom Profile
> **Primary Race:** Mixed / Diverse (No single dominant group)
> **Capital:** Silver-Mill
> **Philosophy:** "All Paths Meet Here."

---

## The Era of Equality

- Humans, Dwarves, Elves, and Dragonborn live together without racial hierarchy.
- Thorne was founded on the principle of integration and shared community.

## The Heartland

- Rolling hills, fertile farmland, forests, plains, and rivers.
- Produces most of the food and timber for the Six Kingdoms.
- Represents the standard way of life.

## The Crossroads

- The center of land trade between the kingdoms.
- A common meeting place for merchants, diplomats, and travelers.
