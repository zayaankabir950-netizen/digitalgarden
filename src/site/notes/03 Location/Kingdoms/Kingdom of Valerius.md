> [!abstract] 🏛️ Kingdom Profile
> **Primary Race:** [High Elves
> **Capital:** The Marble Groves of Valera.
> **Philosophy:** *Eudaimonia* (The Flourishing Life). Peace is not the absence of war, but the presence of balance.
> **Key Landmark:** House Nexum (The House of Continuity)

---

## The Pacifist Elves

- The High Elves swore an oath of non-violence after the **Age of Shien**.
- They refuse to take a life, believing it breaks the "Continuity" they seek to protect.

## Nature and Architecture

- Their cities are grown from living trees and white marble instead of being built.
- Daily life revolves around philosophy, art, and supportive magic.

## House Nexum

- House Nexum specializes in healing and support magic.
- Its mages heal both people and the land.
- Valerius is often chosen for peace talks, where bringing a weapon is considered taboo.

## The Kingdom

- Valerius has no fortresses, prisons, or guard towers.
- Ancient forest magic protects the kingdom by turning unwanted visitors away.
