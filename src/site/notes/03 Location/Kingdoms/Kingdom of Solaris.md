### 📜 The Life of the Manor

In Solaris, time isn't measured by clocks, but by the alignment of the stars.

#### 1. The Astral Dwarfs

Unlike the traditional mountain-dwelling kin of the old eras, the Astral Dwarfs of Solaris are architects of light and glass. They build vertically, reaching toward the heavens. They believe their "Shard" from the Mirror was infused with the silver light of the stars.

- **Appearance:** Many have skin that glimmers like obsidian or hair that seems to trap starlight.
    
- **Craft:** They don't just forge iron; they forge "Star-Steel" and etched glass that can track the orbits of the planets.
    

#### 2. Astrological Governance

The laws of Solaris change with the seasons.

- **The Star-Charts:** Decisions on trade, marriage, and even the opening of the city gates are dictated by the **Great Astrolabe** in the capital.
    
- **The Quiet Life:** Because they follow the stars, the people of Solaris are patient. They don't rush like the Merchant Empires. They wait for the "right alignment" to act.
    

#### 3. House Infedo (The 4th House)


- They use the geometry of the constellations to create their containment fields.
    
- A "Solaris Prison" isn't a dungeon; it’s a room of light where the walls are made of literal starlight that burns anyone who tries to cross the threshold.
    

#### 4. The Vibe of the Land

The landscape is dotted with observatories and "Star-Fields"—vast meadows where the grass turns silver at night. It is the most peaceful of the 6 Kingdoms, acting as a sanctuary for those fleeing the racial friction of the Legacy Four.
