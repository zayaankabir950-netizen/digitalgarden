> [!abstract]  Kingdom Profile
> **Primary Race:** Astral Dwarves
> **Capital:** The Star-Spires of Solis.
> **Philosophy:** "As above, so below." Life is governed by the movement of the celestial bodies.
> **Key Landmark:** House Infedo (The House of Barriers)

---

## The Astral Dwarves

- Astral Dwarves build towering cities of light and glass.
- They believe their Shard was infused with the silver light of the stars.
- They craft **Star-Steel** and enchanted glass used to track the heavens.

## Astrological Governance

- The movement of the stars determines laws, trade, marriages, and other major decisions.
- The **Great Astrolabe** guides the kingdom's actions.

## House Infedo

- House Infedo specializes in barriers and containment magic.
- Their prisons are made from solid starlight instead of stone.

## The Kingdom

- Solaris is filled with observatories and Star-Fields.
- It is the most peaceful of the Six Kingdoms and serves as a sanctuary for those fleeing the racial tensions of the Legacy Four.
