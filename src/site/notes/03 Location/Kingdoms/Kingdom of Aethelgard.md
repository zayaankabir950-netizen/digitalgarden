> [!abstract] Kingdom Profile
> **Primary Race:** Humans
> **Capital:** Aethel-Prime (The White Spires)
> **Philosophy:** Order through Understanding. If you can name it, you can control it.
> **Key Landmark:** House Magica (The House of Fundamentals).

## The Chronicle of the Branch

- During the **Age of Shien**, a traveler accidentally aligned a branch with a mana vein, firing a beam of energy.
- The event proved that magic could be used by anyone with the correct movement, becoming the foundation of Aethelgard's magic.

## Culture of Rules

- Aethelgard believes magic follows strict laws and proper form.
- Their cities are highly organized, and scholars spend decades studying and recording every spell.

## House Magica

- The oldest and most prestigious academy in the Six Kingdoms.
- Students master the basics before being allowed to use a magical focus.

## The Human Element

- Humans act as the administrators of the current peace.
- They authored the **Accord of Shards** and serve as the world's librarians and lawyers.
