> [!abstract] Kingdom Profile
> **Primary Race:** Humans & Dwarves (Symbiotic Society)
>
> **Capital:** The Veiled Basin (The City of Falling Water)
>
> **Philosophy:** "Silence is the Greatest Sanctuary."
>
> **Status:** The Kingdom of Disconnection.

## The Veil of Water

- Nyxos lies inside a massive basin surrounded by endless waterfalls.
- The waterfalls hide and protect the kingdom from the outside world.
- The only way inside is to be guided by a resident Navigator.

## The City of Water

- The capital is built entirely around waterways instead of roads.
- Dwarves maintain the city's foundations and lock systems.
- Humans handle navigation and trade.

## The Library of Frey

- Rumors claim the legendary **Library of Frey** lies beneath the city's central canal.
- The people of Nyxos are known as the **Keepers of the Record**, but never confirm if the library exists.

## The Kingdom

- Nyxos is isolated from the rest of the Six Kingdoms.
- It is known as a quiet place of reflection, largely untouched by the world's conflicts.
