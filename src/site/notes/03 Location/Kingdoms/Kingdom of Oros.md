> [!abstract] Kingdom Profile
> **Primary Race:** Dragonborn
> **Capital:** Drakon-Peak (The Obsidian Bastion)
> **Philosophy:** "Strength is the only truth. Power is the only law."
> **Key Landmark:** House Flotaria (The House of Offense)

## The Dragonborn

- Dragonborn are altered by the nearby mana crystals, making them larger, more aggressive, and resistant to Mana-Burn.
- Every citizen is trained as a soldier, giving Oros the largest military in the Six Kingdoms.

## The Mana Caves

- Oros is built around the **Grand Crystal Caves**, the world's largest source of mana crystals.
- Constant exposure to dense mana leaves the kingdom in a perpetual state of unrest.

## House Flotaria

- The House of Offense trains mages for overwhelming destructive power.
- With an endless supply of mana crystals, efficiency is never a concern.

## Diplomacy

- Oros is the aggressor during the **Era of Coexistence**.
- They oppose the restrictions imposed by the **Legacy Four** and control the world's mana crystal supply.
