### 📜 The Life of the Manor

Oros is a kingdom of jagged stone, glowing blue veins of mana, and the constant sound of marching boots. It is built directly into the side of the **Grand Crystal Caves**.

#### 1. The Dragonborn Legion

The Dragonborn of Oros are physically and magically altered by the proximity to the crystals. They are larger, more aggressive, and naturally resistant to the "Mana-Burn" that would kill a human.

- **The Temperament:** Hot-headed and prideful. They see the diplomacy of Aethelgard and the pacifism of Valerius as weaknesses.
    
- **The Army:** Oros maintains the largest standing military in the 6 Kingdoms. Every citizen is a soldier first, and a laborer second.
    

#### 2. The Mana-Dense Atmosphere

The air in Oros hums. It tastes like ozone and metal. Because they live at the source of the world’s power, everything in Oros is "overcharged."

- **The Caves:** The city is built around the entrance to the primary mana-veins. These crystals aren't just for trade; they are the literal foundation of their architecture.
    
- **The Aggression:** Constant exposure to high-density mana makes the population restless. It’s a kingdom on the brink of a "boil-over" at all times.
    

#### 3. House Flotaria (The 3rd House)

The **House of Offense** is the pride of Oros.

- **The War-Mages:** In this kingdom, a mage isn't a scholar; they are a living siege engine. They train by blasting the cavern walls to expand the city.
    
- **The Efficiency:** Because they have an endless supply of crystals, they never learned to conserve energy. They only know how to output as much destruction as possible.
    

#### 4. The Diplomatic Stance

Oros is the "Aggressor" in the current **Era of Coexistence**. They feel the **Legacy Four** are stifling their growth with rules and treaties. They provide the crystals the world needs, but they do it with a snarl, making sure everyone knows that if the supply stops, the world goes dark.
