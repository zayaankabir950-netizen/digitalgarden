# 📖 Session 1: The Fisherman of Flexim

The journey began in a local tavern, where the trio first assembled. While **El-Farban** and **Dushkar** were busy being ignored by a silent automaton, **Rochat** managed to strike a deal with a large, slimy entity. The "slime boi" tasked them with delivering a crate of wheat to his family, a mission the party accepted before heading out onto a rocky, forested trail.

As the group ascended a steep hill lined with towering trees, they spotted a lone fisherman. In a sudden turn of events, the party decided to ambush him. **Rochat** seized the initiative by throwing a handful of pocket sand directly into the fisherman's eyes. The tactic worked perfectly, leaving the foe blinded and swinging at empty air.

However, the fisherman quickly countered by unleashing a "Domain of Fishes," engulfing the entire party in a spectral aquatic field that buffeted everyone for **2 DMG**. In the chaos, **Dushkar** successfully wove a layer of Magic Armour around himself. It was a life-saving move; moments later, his staff backfired and exploded during a failed casting of Ray of Frost. Thanks to the armour, he remained unhurt by the blast, though the fisherman eventually managed to whack him into a nearby tree for **2 DMG**.

**El-Farban** struggled to find his footing in the arcane struggle, taking a brutal overhead bash from the fisherman for **6 DMG** after the foe teleported directly above him. **Rochat** proved to be the most consistent threat, closing the distance to stab the fisherman for **5 DMG** and following up with a searing Acid Spit that began to melt the target's resolve.

As the battle reached its peak, **Rochat** landed a precise sneak attack that brought the fisherman to the brink of death at **1 HP**. In a final act of spite, the fisherman delivered a fiery kick to **El-Farban**, igniting his leg for a lingering **1 DMG** of burn damage, before vanishing into thin air.

Battered, exhausted, and barely alive, the three heroes limped toward the village of **Flexim** under the cover of night. At the towering gates, they were intercepted by the guards **Bortisimus** and **Fartisumus**. **Rochat** social-engineered their way through by claiming **El-Farban** and **Dushkar** were simply his slaves, and the guards let them pass without further question.

They eventually found refuge at **Weenie Jr’s Tavern and Hotel**, paying 5 smucks for a room. The quarters were strangely decorated with a single large bed covered in rose petals and two bottles of "Shambro Baby Oil" on the dresser. **Rochat** immediately retreated to the bathroom with a pillow and blanket, leaving **El-Farban** and **Dushkar** to share the romantic bed for the night.

**SESSION 1 END**
