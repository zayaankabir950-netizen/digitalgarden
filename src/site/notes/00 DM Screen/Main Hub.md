### ⚙️ How the World Works

- **Magic:** Magic is a fundamental part of the world. It is used for everything from high-level combat to simple household chores. Most people have at least a basic understanding of how mana works.
    
- **The Races:** Diverse and integrated. It is normal to see an Astral Dwarf, a High Elf, and a Dragonborn sharing a meal in any major city tavern.
    
- **The Landscape:** Characterized by sprawling meadows, massive forests, and the ruins of civilizations that came before. The world feels old, large, and full of secrets waiting to be found.
    

---

### 🏛️ Major Pillars

1. **The Passage of Time:** The world moves slowly. Elves and Dwarves live for centuries, while Humans leave their mark through rapid expansion and short, intense lives.
    
2. **The Journey:** Traveling between locations is a core part of life. Roads are well-maintained but the wilds between them remain unpredictable.
    
3. **The Arcane:** Magic isn't just a weapon; it is a field of study. New spells are discovered every year, and old ones are cataloged in massive libraries.
