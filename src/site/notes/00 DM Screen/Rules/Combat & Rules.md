> [!multi-column]
> 
> > [!abstract] ⚔️ Actions in Combat On your turn, you can move and take **one** action:
> > 
> > - **Attack:** Strike with a weapon or spell.
> >     
> > - **Cast a Spell:** See [[00 DM Screen/Rules/Spellcasting Rules\|Spellcasting Rules]].
> >     
> > - **Dash:** Gain extra movement equal to your speed.
> >     
> > - **Disengage:** Moving doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks.
> >     
> > - **Dodge:** Attacks against you have **Disadvantage**; you have **Advantage** on Dex saves.
> >     
> > - **Help:** Give an ally **Advantage** on their next roll.
> >     
> > - **Hide:** Roll Stealth vs. enemy Passive Perception.
> >     
> > - **Ready:** Choose a trigger to take an action later.
> >     
> 
> > [!danger] 💔 Health & Death
> > 
> > - **Damage:** Subtract from Current HP.
> >     
> > - **Dropping to 0:** You fall unconscious.
> >     
> > - **Death Saves:** Roll 1d20 at start of turn.
> >     
> >     - **10+**: Success. (3 wins = Stable).
> >         
> >     - **9 or less**: Failure. (3 losses = 💀).
> >         
> >     - **Nat 20**: Stand up with **1 HP**.
> >         
> >     - **Nat 1**: Counts as **2 Failures**.
> >         
> > - **Instant Death:** If remaining damage equals your Max HP.
> >     
> 
> > [!success] 🛌 Resting
> > 
> > - **Short Rest (1h):** Spend "Hit Dice" to regain HP. See [[00 DM Screen/Rules/Resting Mechanics\|Resting Mechanics]].
> >     
> > - **Long Rest (8h):** Regain all HP and all spent Spell Slots/Resources.
> >

### 🌪️ Conditions & Hazards

|Condition|Effect|
|---|---|
|**Blinded**|You fail sight checks. Attacks against you have Adv / Yours have Disadv.|
|**Charmed**|You can't attack the charmer. They have Adv on social checks vs you.|
|**Frightened**|Disadv on rolls while source is in sight. Cannot move closer to it.|
|**Grappled**|Speed becomes 0. Ends if grappler is incapacitated.|
|**Prone**|You must crawl or spend half movement to stand. Melee attacks vs you have Adv.|
|**Restrained**|Speed 0. Your attacks have Disadv / Enemy attacks have Adv.|

### 🌳 The Environment

> [!example] Cover & Range
> 
> - **Half Cover (+2 AC/Dex):** Hiding behind a small object or creature.
>     
> - **Three-Quarters (+5 AC/Dex):** Peeking around a corner or thick tree.
>     
> - **Total Cover:** You cannot be targeted by direct attacks or spells.
>     
> - **Ranged in Melee:** If an enemy is within 5ft, your ranged attacks have **Disadvantage**.
