# Combat

Combat is turn-based.

## Turn Order
- Roll Initiative.
- Highest goes first.

## Your Turn
- Move.
- Use an Action.
- Use a Bonus Action (if available).
- Reactions happen outside your turn.

## Attacking
- Roll to hit.
- Beat the target's AC.
- Roll damage.

## HP
- 0 HP = Downed.
- Make Death Saves until healed or stabilized.


## Criticals
- Nat 20 = Critical Hit.
- Nat 1 = Critical Miss.
