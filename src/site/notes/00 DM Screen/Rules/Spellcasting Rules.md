> [!info] ✨ The Golden Rule If you cast a spell as a **Bonus Action**, you cannot cast another spell during the same turn, except for a **Cantrip** with a casting time of 1 action.

---

#### 🕒 Casting Times

- **1 Action:** The standard "main" part of your turn.
    
- **1 Bonus Action:** A quick spell (like _Healing Word_ or _Misty Step_).
    
- **Reaction:** Used in response to an event (like _Shield_ or _Counterspell_).
    
- **Ritual:** Takes **10 minutes longer** than usual but doesn't cost a spell slot.
    

#### 🎯 Components & Concentration

- **V (Verbal):** You must be able to speak clearly.
    
- **S (Somatic):** You must have at least one hand free to gesture.
    
- **M (Material):** You need a Component Pouch or a **Spellcasting Focus** (like a staff).
    
- **Concentration:** You can only concentrate on **one** spell at a time. If you take damage, roll a **Constitution Save** (DC 10 or half the damage taken, whichever is higher) to keep the spell going.
    

#### 🎲 The Math

- **Spell Attack:** `1d20 + Ability Mod + Proficiency`
    
- **Spell Save DC:** `8 + Ability Mod + Proficiency`
