Resting is how the party survives the long trek

> [!abstract] ☕ Short Rest (1 Hour)
> 
> - **Healing:** Players can spend **Hit Dice**. Roll the die (e.g., 1d6 for Wizard, 1d8 for Thief) + Constitution modifier.
>     
> - **Resources:** Some classes regain abilities (like a Wizard's _Arcane Recovery_).
>     
> - **Limit:** You can only spend Hit Dice if you have them left!
>     

> [!success] 🛌 Long Rest (8 Hours)
> 
> - **Full Recovery:** All HP is restored to maximum.
>     
> - **Spell Slots:** All slots are regained.
>     
> - **Hit Dice Recovery:** Players regain **half** of their total max Hit Dice.
>     
> - **Requirements:** A player can only benefit from **one** Long Rest in a 24-hour period. Must have at least 1 HP at the start.
>
