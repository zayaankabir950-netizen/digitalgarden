More spells will accumulate depending on the users level and items
##### Cantrips
can be used at will.

- **Fire Bolt:** 1d10
- **Ray of Frost:** 1d8, and reduces target's movement speed by 10 feet
- **Mage Hand:** You summon a floating hand up to 30 feet away to safely interact with objects remotely
- **Arcane Mastery:** Once per short rest recover 1 mana slot
#### Unlocked spells
Unlocked/Learnt spells. Take 1 mana slot by base.
- **Magic Missile:** 3d4 + 3, this attack never misses and automatically hits the target
- **Shield:** +5 to your Armor Class (AC) until your next turn when an enemy attacks you (Can ==only== be used as reaction in battles)
- **Burning Hands:** 3d6 to all enemies in a 15-foot cone in front of you.
