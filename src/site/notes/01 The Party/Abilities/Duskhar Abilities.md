**Cantrips (At-Will):**

- **Ray of Frost:** 1d8 Cold damage + reduce enemy speed by 10ft.
    
- **Light:** Touch an object; it glows for 1 hour.
    
- **Mage Hand:** Create a floating, invisible hand to move objects up to 30ft.
    
- **Shocking Grasp:** Melee spell attack. 1d8 Lightning. Target can't take reactions.
    
- **Minor Illusion:** Create a sound or an image of an object within 30ft.
    

**Level 1 Spells:**

- **Mage Armor:** AC becomes 13 + Dex (8 hours).
    
- **Sleep:** Roll 5d8; total is the HP of creatures you knock out.
