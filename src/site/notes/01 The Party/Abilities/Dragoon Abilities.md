**Attack Options:**

- **Knife Throat:** Melee Attack. 1d4 + Dex damage. If you have Advantage, add your **Sneak Attack** (+1d6).
    
- **Acid Breath:** 15ft Cone. 2d6 Acid damage. (Dex Save for half damage).
    
- **Off-Hand Shank:** Bonus Action. If you attacked with your main action, you can strike again with a second dagger (1d4 damage, no Dex bonus).
    
- **Pocket Sand:** (Fast Hands) Use a bonus action to fling debris. Target makes a Con Save or is **Blinded** until the start of your next turn.
    

**Thief Maneuvers:**

- **Cunning Action:** Use a Bonus Action to **Dash** (double move), **Disengage** (safe move), or **Hide** (Stealth check).
    
- **Fast Hands:** Bonus Action to pick a lock, disarm a trap, or use a healing potion on an ally.
    
- **Second-Story Work:** You can climb walls at full speed and jump an extra distance equal to your Dex modifier.
