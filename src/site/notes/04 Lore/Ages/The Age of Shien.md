> [!danger] 💎 Era Summary **The Catalyst:** Discovery of the **Deep-Glass Caves** (Mana-Crystals). **Economy:** Rise of the Merchant Empires. **Social:** Systematic enslavement of non-human "Shards" for crystal extraction.

---

### 📜 The Chronicles of Shien

#### 1. The Crystal Rush

Everything changed when the first vein of blue mana-crystal was found in the salt-flats. These crystals weren't just pretty; they were "frozen power." If you cracked one, it could heat a house for a month. If you ground it into powder, you could fuel a war.

#### 2. The Merchant Empires (The Top 4)

Four massive trade families consolidated power and declared themselves Kingdoms. They didn't care about "thrones"—they cared about the **monopoly**.

- **[[03 Location/Kingdoms/Kingdom of Aethelgard\|Kingdom of Aethelgard]]**: The first to industrialize the "crushing mills."
    
- **[[03 Location/Kingdoms/Kingdom of Oros\|Kingdom of Oros]]**: Controlled the shipping lanes for raw crystals.
    
- **[[03 Location/Kingdoms/Kingdom of Valerius\|Kingdom of Valerius]]**: The "Security" wing (Mercenaries).
    
- **[[03 Location/Kingdoms/Kingdom of Nyxos\|Kingdom of Nyxos]]**: The "Bank" that funded the slave ships.
    

#### 3. The "Shard" Labor

To keep profits high, the empires needed cheap, expendable labor. Because non-humans (Elves, Dwarves, Dragonborn) were "born of the Mirror," the Merchants argued they weren't people—they were just **living extensions of the crystals**.

- **The Mines:** Thousands of non-humans were forced into the lightless deep-glass caves to haul out raw mana.
    
- **The Edo-Style Cities:** The surface looked beautiful—clean, structured, and wealthy—but it was built entirely on the backs of the enslaved "Shards" beneath the floorboards.
    

#### 4. "Modernization"

This era saw the first **Mana-Rail** and **Steam-Loom**, but they were all powered by crystals pulled from the blood and sweat of the non-human races. This is the "Invention Age" your world remembers, but the history books try to hide the cost.
