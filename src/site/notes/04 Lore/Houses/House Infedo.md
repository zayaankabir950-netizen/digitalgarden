> [!abstract] 🛡️ Academy Profile
>  **The Motto:** _"The Bound Cannot Break."_
>  **The Focus:** **Abjuration and Sealing.** The complex geometry of starlight barriers, containment fields, and absolute imprisonment.
>   **The People:** Stoic, vigilant, and often morally gray. They are the royal jailers and the final line of defense for the 6 Kingdoms.

### 📜 The Hall of the Seal

#### 1. The Geometry of the Stars

Located in the astrological heart of Solaris, House Infedo teaches that the universe is held together by invisible, unbreakable lines. By mimicking these constellations, a mage can "lock" a space out of time or physical reality.

- **Starlight Barriers:** Their shields aren't just walls of force; they are intricate webs of light that absorb impact and redirect it into the stars.
    
- **The Absolute Prison:** Taking inspiration from the "binding" arts, they can trap a target in a localized dimension—a pocket of space where time stands still and escape is physically impossible without the caster's key.
    

#### 2. The Royal Executioners

Because their magic is the only thing capable of holding high-level mages or legendary threats, House Infedo is deeply connected to the high royalty of the Legacy Four.

- **The Silent Hand:** When a king needs a rival removed without a public execution, they call an Infedo "Executioner." The target isn't killed; they are simply vanished into a prism or a sealed vault forever.
    
- **The Royal Guard:** Many graduates serve as the personal shields for the 6 Kingdoms' leaders, standing as silent sentinels who can drop a dome of starlight at a moment's notice.
    

#### 3. The Weight of the Key

The mages of this house carry a heavy burden. They are the only ones who know how to open the prisons they create.

- **The Vibe:** They are often seen as cold or detached. To be a master of Infedo is to be a warden of secrets.
    
- **The Civil War Tension:** In the current era, they are the ones building the "Great Walls" and the containment zones that keep the different races separated in the more volatile border-lands.
    

#### 4. The Mirror-Vaults

The academy itself is a labyrinth of mirrors and containment cells. It is said that some of the greatest enemies of the **Age of Shien** are still trapped in the basement of Solaris, frozen in starlight and forgotten by the world.
