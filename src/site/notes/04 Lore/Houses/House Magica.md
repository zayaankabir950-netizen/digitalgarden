> [!abstract] 📜 Academy Profile
>  **The Motto:** "Structure is the Vessel of Power."
>   **The People:** Disciplined, principled, and deeply committed to the public good. They are the world's librarians, judges, and protectors.
>    **The Entry Requirement:** Three years of "Silent Study"—mastering the history of the Branch-Strike before ever being permitted to channel mana.

---

### 📜 The Hall of Principles

#### 1. The Mastery of Form

At House Magica, magic is treated as a precise physical science. Because the kingdom was founded on that one specific "swing of a branch," the scholars here believe every spell has a "Perfect Motion."

- **Somatic Excellence:** Students spend hours in the open courtyards repeating the same arm movements. A centimeter of deviance in a flick of the wrist is considered a failure that must be corrected.
    
- **Mental Discipline:** They teach that a chaotic mind leads to a chaotic spell. Every student is expected to be steady, reliable, and predictable in their actions.
    

#### 2. The Main Principles

While other houses specialized later, Magica remains the keeper of the **Universal Foundations**. You don't come here to learn "tricks"; you come here to learn how magic _works_:

- **Mana-Flow:** Learning how to pull energy from the atmosphere without "stinging" the soul.
    
- **Transmutation Basics:** Changing the properties of simple matter (turning lead to copper, water to ice).
    
- **Arcane Script:** The study of the written symbols that "lock" a spell into place.
    

#### 3. The Keepers of the Peace

The House doesn't just teach magic; it teaches **Civic Duty**.

- **The Code of Conduct:** Mages of this house are the primary supporters of the **Accord of Shards**. They believe that unregulated magic led to the greed of the merchant empires, and they act as the moral compass for the 6 Kingdoms.
    
- **The Advisors:** You’ll find Magica graduates serving as the lawyers and advisors in the Legacy Four, ensuring that the rules of the current peace are followed to the letter.
    

#### 4. The First Vault

Deep beneath the academy lies the most heavily guarded room in Aethelgard. It contains the original records of the "Branch-Strike" and ancient fragments of the Mirror. The air in the vault is said to be so heavy with the "weight of truth" that it's nearly impossible for a dishonest person to breathe comfortably inside.
