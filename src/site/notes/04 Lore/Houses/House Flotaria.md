> [!abstract] ⚔️ Academy Profile
>  **The Motto:** _"The Strike is the Final Answer."_ 
>  **The Focus:** **Absolute Offense.** The study of high-output mana strikes, destructive resonance, and overwhelming force.
>   **The People:** Noteworthy, ambitious, and often reckless. They are known as the "Glass Cannons"—famous for their power but fragile in a long-term engagement.

### 📜 The Hall of the Strike

#### 1. Crystal-Resonance Training

Unlike House Magica, which draws from the atmosphere, Flotaria mages are trained in "Deep-Saturation." They are raised around high-grade mana-crystals to expand their internal reserves.

- **Mana Efficiency:** By living in crystal-rich environments, they learn to channel massive amounts of energy with minimal effort. Their spells aren't just powerful; they are "pressurized."
    
- **The High Reserve:** A Flotaria mage can hold three times the mana of a standard scholar, allowing them to unleash devastating barrages that can level a fortification in a single heartbeat.
    

#### 2. The Noteworthy Mages

Graduates of this house are the ones recorded in the history books. They are the most visible and feared mages in the 6 Kingdoms.

- **The Deterrent:** In the current **Era of Coexistence**, the mere presence of a Flotaria mage in a border-town is often enough to keep the "Civil War" tension from boiling over.
    
- **The Reputation:** They are celebrated for their ability to end a conflict instantly, though their methods are often loud and destructive.
    

#### 3. The Forgotten Art of Defense

The biggest flaw of this house is their total abandonment of protective magic. They have forgotten how to cast a basic shield or weave a healing thread.

- **The Philosophy:** Why waste mana on a wall when you can vaporize the person attacking it?
    
- **The Reliance:** Because they have no defensive capabilities, they are forced to work in teams. They rely entirely on a **House Infedo** barrier or a **House Nexum** healer to keep them safe while they "charge" their next strike.
    

#### 4. The Testing Ranges

The academy is built into the jagged cliffs of Oros. It isn't a quiet library; it’s a series of reinforced blast-zones. The air always smells of ozone and burnt stone, and the students are encouraged to push their limits until their eyes glow with the blue light of the crystals.
