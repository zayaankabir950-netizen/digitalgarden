> [!abstract] 🤝 Academy Profile
>  **The Motto:** _"The Strength of One is the Life of All."_
>   **The Identity:** **The Supporters.** They believe their magic is incomplete until it is used to elevate someone else. 
>   **The People:** Patient, empathetic, and observant. They are the counselors, the nurturers, and the stabilizing force in any group.

### 📜 The Hall of Continuity

#### 1. The Philosophy of the Link

At Nexum, students are taught that mana is a communal resource. They don't cast "at" things; they cast "for" people.

- **The Supportive Bond:** They specialize in magic that flows through others—enhancing a warrior's strength, sharpening an explorer's eyes, or shielding a friend from exhaustion.
    
- **Non-Combatants:** Most Nexum mages refuse to carry offensive weapons. They believe that if they are fighting, they aren't focusing enough on the safety of those around them.
    

#### 2. The Art of Restoration

Their healing isn't just "closing a wound." It’s about restoring the natural flow of a person’s life.

- **Mending the Shard:** They study how to heal the deep, spiritual exhaustion left over from the labor camps of the Shien era.
    
- **Continuity of Care:** They don't just "fix and leave." They believe in the long-term process of helping a person or a community return to their full potential.
    

#### 3. The Life of the Supporter

Nexum mages are often the "heart" of any traveling party or diplomatic mission.

- **The Listeners:** They are trained in mediation and empathy. In the current era of "Civil War" tension, they are the ones trying to talk people down from a fight.
    
- **The Quiet Presence:** They find their purpose in the success of others. If a Kingdom prospers or a friend achieves a goal, the Nexum mage considers their magic a success.
    

#### 4. The Fountain of Flow

The center of the academy in Valerius is a massive, tiered fountain where mana-infused water falls in a continuous, unbreakable loop. It represents their belief that life and magic must always be in motion, supporting the next level down.
