### 📜 Academy Details

- **[[04 Lore/Houses/House Magica\|House Magica]] (The First House):** Being the oldest, it is the most prestigious and traditional. Every mage starts here to learn how to "feel" mana before specializing. Its placement in **Aethelgard** keeps it close to the old centers of power.
    
- **[[04 Lore/Houses/House Nexum\|House Nexum]]:** Located in the militaristic or security-focused **Valerius**, this house likely evolved to keep the kingdom's "Security" forces alive during the Shien era crystal rushes.
    
- **[[04 Lore/Houses/House Flotaria\|House Flotaria]]:** The "Hammer" of the academic world. _(Note: You didn't specify a kingdom for this one—should it be in **Oros** or **Nyxos**?)_
    
- **[[04 Lore/Houses/House Infedo\|House Infedo]] (The Newest House):** Founded in the neutral territory of **Solaris**. It represents the shift away from the "Attack" magic of the older eras toward a magic of protection and peace—or perhaps for keeping high-profile prisoners of the "Legacy Four" contained.
